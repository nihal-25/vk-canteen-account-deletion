Request Account Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your account with vk, please send a request to: nihal6mn@gmail.com

Data Deleted Upon Account Removal

  • Profile information (name, email, username)
  • Authentication data (login credentials)
  • Order history and saved cart items
  • App preferences and settings

Data Retained

  • Transaction records may be retained for up to 90 days for legal, tax, or financial compliance purposes.
  • Error and crash logs may be stored for up to 30 days to improve app stability.
  • Security-related data (e.g., fraud prevention logs) may be kept for a maximum of 180 days.

Retention Period

All retained data is automatically deleted after the retention period expires. No data is shared with third parties beyond legal or compliance requirements.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at nihal6mn@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: “Account Deletion Request”.
  3. We will verify your identity via your registered email.
  4. Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.
